About

US

IEEE Computer Society Chandigarh University was formed as a student augmented society in 2022. It emerges out with an aim to grab every possible opportunity for its student aspirers and be a well-known name for computing innovations and algorithm.

It has been a privilege to be a hub for Developing computational paradigms in science and engineering. It stands responsible for organizing various hackathons, seminars, bootcamps and a variety of workshops to encourage its student to inspire and aspire simultaneously with new skill acquirement. IEEE CS CU looks forward to the bright future waiting for its members in their success paths.

Our

Vision

Our strategy is to provide value to the whole community. It is not just limited to IEEE CS team but to whole IEEE CUSB team. We plan to engage more students and early career professionals and also help them to build their resume. Lead our society’s way to the new technical areas and to maintain focus on diversity and inclusion.

Our

Mission

IEEE COMPUTER SOCIETY aims to take a hold of all probable opportunities for its students. Our society is dedicated to empower new innovations, technologies and ideas. We strive to be the leading contributor of technical information, community services, personalized support and experience, helping our members to grow. We also aim to provide a healthy and thriving network of officials to them and a comfortable environment.
